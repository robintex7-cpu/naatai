Your Measurements

All measurements in centimetres. Keep the tape firm and start from the beginning of the tape.

For the most accurate fit, have someone else take your measurements while you stand straight.

How to measure

  • Waist: Measure around your natural waistline, above the hip bone.
  • Hip: Measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat.
  • Crotch: Sit on a flat chair, measure from waist down to the chair seat.
  • Thigh: Measure around the fullest part of your thigh.
  • Knee: Measure around the knee while slightly bent.
  • Length: Measure from waist to the bottom of your ankle bone.
