How to read this chart

  • Zones: wait for price to reach a green demand / red supply zone — don't chase in the middle.
  • Delta & CVD: at a demand zone, look for red candles but rising CVD (sellers absorbed) → long clue. Opposite at supply.
  • Divergence: price makes a new high but CVD doesn't → buyers weakening (marked ▼ on chart).
  • OI: price ↑ + OI ↑ = new longs (strong). Price ↑ + OI ↓ = short covering (weak rally).
  • Funding: very positive = crowded longs → squeeze risk. Very negative = crowded shorts.
  • Always set a stop-loss from the calculator before entering. Risk ≤ 1–2% per trade.

How to take a trade — step by step

1Wait for the candle to CLOSE
The BUY triangle only prints when the signal candle closes. Never enter mid-candle — the numbers are still changing until the close.
2Check delta ≥ +20% on real volume
Dashboard "Delta" row must be strongly green (buyers dominant) and the candle's volume above its 50-bar average — big delta on a quiet candle is noise. For a SELL you want ≤ −20%.
3HTF trend must agree
"HTF trend (4h)" row says UP → longs only. Says DOWN → shorts only. Trading with the 4-hour trend filters out most losing signals.
4Fib / zone confluence
Best entries happen where price bounces off the 0.5–0.618 fib zone or a demand/supply zone. Price holding the level + positive delta = buyers defending support.
5Enter with the printed SL/TP
Enter at the signal candle's close. Stop-loss and take-profit are printed on the chart (SL = 1.5×ATR, TP = 2R). Risk 1% of account — use the position size calculator.
6Heikin Ashi confirms & manages
Bottom panel flipped green = higher-timeframe momentum agrees. Hold to TP/SL; exit early only if the HA panel flips red against you.
Managing the trade
— +1R in profit (price moved the same distance as your stop) → move stop-loss to break-even. The trade can no longer lose.
— Exit at TP or SL (mechanical, preferred), on an opposite signal, or if the Heikin Ashi panel flips against you.
— Typical hold on the 15m chart: 1–6 hours. On 1h: 4h–2 days. On 4h: 1–7 days.
— Skip the trade if any of steps 1–4 or 6 is missing, or within 15 min of major news (CPI, FOMC, NFP).
